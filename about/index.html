About

2020-01-20 — Written by Kacper

Kacper is a Ph.D. candidate in the Electrical and Computer Engineering department at Boston University advised by Prof. Wenchao Li. His research focus has been on the design and implementation of reliable cyber-physical systems and security for multi-robot systems. Kacper earned a B.Sc. in Computer Science & Engineering and a B.Sc. in Mathematics from Ohio State University in 2016.
